Step 4 · Carbonara
4
Combine off the heat
Pull the pan off. Wait 30 seconds. Add hot pasta, toss hard. Pour the egg paste in gradually — keep tossing. The steam from the pasta does the cooking.
Look for this
Glossy and pale golden coating every strand. Grainy means the pan was too hot. Wet and loose means keep tossing — it'll come together.
Eggs scramble at 70°C. Off-heat is the only safe zone — the residual warmth in the pasta is your cooking medium, not the flame.

Step 5 · Final
5
Plate and serve — now
Move fast. Carbonara doesn't wait. Twist into a nest with tongs, lay a few guanciale pieces over the top. Grate Pecorino generously. Crack black pepper — a lot of it.
Serve within
60 seconds from pan to plate. Pasta absorbs sauce as it sits — don't plate into a cold bowl.
The pepper is essential, not decoration. Carbonara means "of the coal." The pepper is the coal. Be generous — it's not a garnish.
