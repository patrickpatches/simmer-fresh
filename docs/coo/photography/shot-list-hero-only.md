---

## How to read this list

- **Hero angle:** 30° is the default. Overhead is noted where it serves the dish better (e.g. salads, flat breads, layered dishes).
- **Surface:** Dark surfaces are first preference per the Dark Dramatic direction.
- **Alt text:** Copy directly into the recipe's hero `photo_url` alt field.
